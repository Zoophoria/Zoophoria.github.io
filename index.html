About Us

Animals are our passion. We strive to not only educate the public on wildlife, but also to assist organizations in conservation efforts. Currently we utilize clothing campaigns to assist with the protection of different species. Our campaigns are constantly changing and each one has a focus on the conservation efforts of a specific animal. For every article of clothing sold, 15% of the profits are donated to organizations that work in effort to conserve and protect the animals featured on the clothing. Our current campaign can always be seen by simply scrolling down on this page.
